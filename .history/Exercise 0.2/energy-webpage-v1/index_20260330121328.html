Energy Consumption in Australia

Energy consumption in Australian households has increased significantly over the past decade due to the growing use of electrical appliances. Devices such as televisions, refrigerators, air conditioners, and washing machines contribute a large portion of total electricity usage. In Australia, energy efficiency is an important factor when purchasing appliances, as it directly affects both electricity costs and environmental impact. Government initiatives such as energy rating labels help consumers make informed decisions when selecting appliances. This website explores the energy usage of common household appliances, with a focus on televisions, to better understand their impact on energy consumption in the Australian market.

Television Energy Usage

Modern televisions vary significantly in energy consumption depending on size and technology. LED TVs are generally more energy-efficient compared to older plasma models.
